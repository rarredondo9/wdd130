Corn Cake

This is a very smooth and moist cake that you will love. Such a different dessert and so easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of sweet kernel corn (about 600 g)

    2 cans of condensed milk (about 750 g)

    6 eggs

    4 teaspoons of butter

    2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour

    • Preparation

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 350F. In a blender add 1 can of sweet kernel corn, 1 can of condensed milk, 3 eggs, 2 tablespoons of butter and a tablespoon of all-purpose flour. Use the pulse setting on your blender and pulse until the ingredients have combined. (Do not blend).

    2. Add the batter on a 7-inch pan. Then repeat the process with the rest of the ingredients and add the rest to the pan.

    3. Bake for 45 minutes and then let it rest for an hour. Enjoy!
