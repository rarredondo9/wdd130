About Us

At Avia’s Cooking is all about bringing people together and creating lasting memories. We are no experts, but we cook with love. Here you will be able to find a collection of cherished family recipes that will inspire and satisfy your taste buds. Our family believes in the power of simplicity. For us the most satisfying meals come from humble ingredients. Our recipes are crafted with love, focusing on using accessible ingredients and easy to follow instructions ensuring extraordinary meals. Here you will find my mother Lorena’s recipes and y mother-in-law Aury’s recipes. Probably some of mine too. We want our family to access these recipes in the future and get a taste of home. With us sharing our recipes we are also inviting you to our home, so WELCOME!
